SAFE BY DEFAULT

SignalOnly ships with auth and payment domains (Google, Apple, Microsoft, GitHub, Stripe, PayPal, ...) on a permanent shield-exclusion list. Page-world fingerprint patches only run on sites you explicitly opt in. Header rewriting and third-party cookie stripping skip those domains regardless of settings.

01 / NETWORK

Network

WebRTC / DNS leak blockingHides the local interface IP. "Soft" preserves WebRTC; "Strict" disables non-proxied UDP and pairs with a SOCKS proxy.
Header normalizationSets DNT, Sec-GPC, and Accept-Language to the active profile. Skips trusted auth and payment domains.
Spoof User-Agent header (advanced)Risky. Google and many SSO providers refuse sign-in when the HTTP UA does not match the Chrome client hints. The page-world UA spoof is unaffected.
Strip third-party cookies (advanced)Removes the Cookie header from third-party requests. Will break SSO/OAuth on every site that doesn't have its identity provider in the trusted list.
Strip ETag / Last-ModifiedDisrupts cache-based tracking on third-party requests.

03 / STORAGE

Storage

When enabled, localStorage / IndexedDB / Cache / BroadcastChannel are namespaced with a per-profile salt. Sites you assign profiles to will be signed out on toggle. Off by default.

Block Service WorkersOff by default. Gmail, Docs, Meet, and many PWAs depend on registering one.

04 / PII SHIELD

PII Shield

Autofill suppressionTells Chrome to disable address and payment autofill.
Referrer trimmingReduces cross-site origin disclosure. Some auth flows require referrers; verify before enabling.
Block Topics / FLEDGE / measurementDisables Privacy Sandbox advertising APIs.
Auto-clear cookies on profile switchAutomatically removes cookies when a site's profile is changed. Recommended for real isolation.
Cookie expiry capCaps first-party cookie lifetime on assigned sites. Prevents persistent tracking cookies from surviving indefinitely. Set to 0 to disable.

05 / SENSORS

Sensors

Geolocation, battery, motion, orientation, and ambient sensors are blocked on assigned sites.

06 / BEHAVIOR

Behavior Noise

Adds small pointer-coordinate jitter. Timing APIs are left untouched to avoid breaking authentication, signed cookies, and time-based login flows.
